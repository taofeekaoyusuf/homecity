Top three activities to do at Ibadan

Attend a Show or a Concert

The Bower Tower is a great place to spend your night out with family, friends, and loved ones, with great delight.

Go for Shopping at the Cocoa House Building

The Cocoa House is perfect for shopping, getting some nice food and drinks, or chilling out with your families.

Get your memories inprinted while visiting the Mapo Hall.

Mapo Hall, built 1932, has been a memory hall for many dignitaries around the World, don't be left out.
